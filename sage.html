What is Sage?

Sage is a global software company that provides accounting, payroll, enterprise resource planning (ERP), and financial management solutions for businesses of all sizes. It is widely used in industries such as manufacturing, retail, healthcare, construction, and professional services.

With over 40 years of experience and millions of customers worldwide, Sage is known for robust financial management tools, compliance support, and scalability.

Key Features & Benefits of Sage

Accounting & Financial Management

Sage Accounting, Sage 50, Sage Intacct

Sage offers powerful cloud-based and desktop accounting software to help businesses manage finances, automate invoicing, track cash flow, and ensure tax compliance.

Key Features:

  • Accounts Payable & Receivable – Track and manage invoices and payments
  • Bank Reconciliation – Automatically match transactions
  • Tax Compliance – VAT, GST, and sales tax automation
  • Multi-Currency Support – Ideal for international businesses
  • Real-Time Financial Reporting – Customizable dashboards for financial insights

How It Helps Businesses:

  • Reduces errors in financial reporting
  • Saves time by automating bookkeeping and tax calculations
  • Helps businesses stay compliant with local tax regulations

Payroll & HR Management

Sage Payroll, Sage HR

Sage provides payroll and human resource management solutions to automate employee payments, tax deductions, and compliance.

Key Features:

  • Automated Payroll Processing – Manage salaries, bonuses, and overtime
  • Tax & Compliance Support – Ensures legal payroll tax deductions
  • Employee Self-Service Portal – Employees can access pay stubs and leave requests
  • Performance Management – Track employee goals and performance

How It Helps Businesses:

  • Reduces time spent on manual payroll calculations
  • Ensures accurate salary and tax payments
  • Improves HR management with employee self-service options

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

Sage 100, Sage 300, Sage X3

Sage ERP solutions integrate multiple business processes (accounting, inventory, sales, HR) into a centralized system.

Key Features:

  • Inventory & Supply Chain Management – Track stock levels, orders, and shipments
  • Production & Manufacturing Management – Plan and monitor production schedules
  • Financial Forecasting & Budgeting – Helps businesses plan for growth
  • Multi-Location & Multi-Currency Support – Suitable for international businesses

How It Helps Businesses:

  • Increases operational efficiency
  • Provides real-time visibility into business performance
  • Helps businesses scale by centralizing critical business processes

Sage CRM

Customer Relationship Management

Sage CRM helps businesses track customer interactions, manage sales pipelines, and improve customer support.

Key Features:

  • Lead & Opportunity Management – Track and nurture potential customers
  • Email & Marketing Automation – Send personalized emails and promotions
  • Customer Support & Case Management – Streamline issue resolution
  • Reporting & Analytics – Gain insights into customer behavior and sales trends

How It Helps Businesses:

  • Boosts customer retention and satisfaction
  • Automates lead tracking and sales follow-ups
  • Improves team collaboration with centralized customer data

Sage Business Cloud

Cloud-Based Solutions

Sage offers cloud-based solutions for businesses that need access to accounting, payroll, and ERP software from anywhere.

Key Features:

  • Multi-Device Access – Works on desktops, tablets, and smartphones
  • Data Backup & Security – Cloud storage with encryption for security
  • Seamless Integration – Connects with banking systems, e-commerce platforms, and tax agencies

How It Helps Businesses:

  • Enables remote work and mobile access
  • Provides real-time financial insights
  • Ensures data security and automatic backups

Fixed Assets Management

Sage Fixed Assets

Businesses using Sage can track, manage, and depreciate assets like machinery, vehicles, and equipment.

Key Features:

  • Asset Lifecycle Tracking – Monitor purchases, usage, and disposal
  • Depreciation Calculation – Automates depreciation schedules
  • Tax Compliance & Audit Support – Ensures regulatory compliance

How It Helps Businesses:

  • Helps businesses maximize asset value
  • Reduces the risk of lost or untracked assets
  • Ensures compliance with tax and financial reporting requirements

Analytics & Reporting

Sage Intelligence

Sage provides powerful business intelligence tools to help businesses make data-driven decisions.

Key Features:

  • Custom Financial Reports – Balance sheets, profit & loss, and cash flow statements
  • AI-Powered Insights – Identify trends and business growth opportunities
  • Real-Time Data Visualization – Dashboards for better decision-making

How It Helps Businesses:

  • Helps business owners track performance
  • Automates report generation, saving time
  • Provides AI-driven recommendations for financial planning
