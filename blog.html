Future of Finance Automation in Africa: How Zoho Is Driving Change

By Damigo Automation Team Published June 2025
Zoho's localized solutions are transforming African businesses

The African business landscape is rapidly embracing digital transformation, with finance automation at the heart of this evolution. Across the continent, small and medium enterprises (SMEs) and large corporations alike are seeking tools that streamline operations, ensure compliance, and drive growth—all while navigating complex regulatory environments.

Zoho's Strategic Investments

Zoho is at the forefront of this movement, actively supporting African businesses with robust, cloud-based finance solutions tailored to local needs. Recent investments and partnerships underscore Zoho’s commitment:

In 2025, Zoho announced a $15 million investment in Zimbabwe to accelerate digital transformation for SMEs and large organizations. This initiative is part of a broader strategy to lower technical barriers and empower businesses through affordable, innovative cloud solutions.

Localized Solutions for Africa

In South Africa, Zoho has achieved remarkable growth—43% in new customers within months of launching its local finance and operations suite. The platform now offers fully localized versions of:

  • Zoho Books with VAT compliance
  • Automated invoicing in local currencies
  • Real-time financial dashboards
Why This Matters for Kenyan Businesses

These updates enable companies to focus on growth while staying ahead of regulatory changes, reducing manual work by up to 60%.

Collaborative Approach

Zoho’s partnership with Loita Business provides SMEs in Zimbabwe, Zambia, and South Africa with:

Software subscription credits
Quarterly training workshops
Local implementation support
Compliance documentation
