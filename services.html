Automation Process

Our proven 5-step process to transform your business

1

Discovery & Consultation

What Happens?

  • Understanding the client's business, challenges, and pain points.
  • Discussing which processes can be automated (e.g., invoicing, CRM, inventory).
  • Providing an initial assessment
2

Needs Analysis & Customization

What Happens?

  • The company analyzes workflows and identifies inefficiencies.
  • Mapping out how automation tools (e.g., Zoho, Sage ) can integrate.
  • Customizing a tailored solution for the client's business.
3

Implementation & Integration

What Happens?

  • The automation software is installed, configured, and integrated into the client's system.
  • Connecting tools like Zoho CRM, accounting software, or inventory management.
  • Data migration (if needed).
4

Training & Onboarding

What Happens?

  • Hands-on training sessions for employees.
  • Providing guides, video tutorials, or live support.
  • Ensuring smooth adoption of the new system.
5

Ongoing Support & Optimization

What Happens?

  • Monitoring system performance and making improvements.
  • 24/7 support for troubleshooting and updates.
  • Collecting feedback from clients to optimize processes.
