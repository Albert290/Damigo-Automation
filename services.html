Our Services

Need Analysis For Automation

What Happens?

  • Understanding client's business, processes, and challenges.
  • Interacting with users of the current system to understand their needs and pain points.
2

Implementation, Data Migration & Customization

What Happens?

  • Configuring the Zoho system precisely to user requirements.
  • Executing careful and verified data migration from legacy systems.
  • Rigorous testing and client consultation for necessary customizations.
3

Go-Live and Training

What Happens?

  • Utilizing current sample data to test the Go-Live phase and onboard users smoothly.
  • Training users effectively with a mock test environment, adapting as needed.
  • Monitoring the Go-Live phase closely, evaluating performance, and responding promptly to queries.
