Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the typical automation implementation take? ▼

Implementation timelines vary depending on the complexity and scope of your project. Simple automations can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while more complex enterprise solutions may take 2-3 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline specific to your requirements.

Do you offer support after implementation? ▼

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive post-implementation support and maintenance for all our automation solutions. Our support packages include regular updates, troubleshooting, and technical assistance. We offer different support tiers to match your specific needs and budget.

Can you integrate with our existing systems? ▼

Yes, our automation solutions are designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing systems. We have extensive experience integrating with a wide range of platforms and applications beyond just Sage and Zoho. During our discovery phase, we'll assess your current tech stack and design a solution that works harmoniously with your existing infrastructure.

What ROI can we expect from automation? ▼

Most of our clients see ROI within 3-6 months of implementing our automation solutions. Typical benefits include 70-85% reduction in manual data entry time, 40-60% decrease in processing errors, and 25-35% improvement in overall productivity. We can provide industry-specific case studies during our consultation to give you a better idea of expected returns for your business.

Do you offer training for our team? ▼

Yes, we provide comprehensive training as part of our implementation process. Our training includes hands-on sessions, documentation, and video tutorials tailored to different user roles within your organization. We ensure your team is confident and proficient with the new automation systems before we complete the handover.
